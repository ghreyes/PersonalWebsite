Lift

Senior project made for our 170 series. Our team of 6 designed, developed, and deployed a game from the ground up using Unreal Engine 4. It is a third person platformer where the goal is to make it to the finish as fast as you can. Stay on clouds to gain boost and use boost to fly. Bonus points are added for airtime and fast finish times. Navigate to Lift's webpage by clicking the image above.

Banner

Banner, the Budget Planner, is a project made for Web Design 183. Our team of 4 designed and implemented Banner as a budget planning tool to help users visually see how much money they are earning compared to how much they are spending and how long it will take to reach a goal. Banner also compares the user's data to the U.S. national average shown at the bottom right. Click the image above to navigate to Banner's webpage. Free account required.

Patient Pond

Patient Pond is a digital interactive conceptual art piece done for a Film and Digital Media class. Initially, users are shown an empty pond. As users click, a ping will appear at the location you clicked. After a few seconds, a fish appears from off-screen and travels to the ping before swimming around for a bit and eventually finding their way off-screen again. The piece is meant to convey the message that sometimes your actions do not have immediate reactions.

Battleship

The Mobile Applications final project was a simplistic version of the classic board game Battleship. This mobile app made use of user interaction and server communication to synchronize the players' game states after every move. This version requires both players to input the same game code on their devices to play one another.

Exercycle

Exercycle is a prototype built for a User Experience class. The purpose was to design a VR exercise experience that was more enjoyable, relaxing, or better overall. The design featured a bike held in place with a stand with a dynamo attatched to the wheel. The dynamo was wired to an Arduino bored which fed into a laptop. Our team of 4 designed a virtual environment that the user would bike through by peddling the immobile bicycle.
